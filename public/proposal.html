Project overview
Summary

Design and build a lean faceless video SaaS MVP that lets users generate vertical videos (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) from simple prompts and pay a monthly subscription for access.

The focus is speed to market: launch quickly, onboard your first users, and validate recurring revenue before expanding features.

Scope & deliverables
Phase 1 – MVP

Phase 1 includes everything required to onboard paying users and deliver value:

  • Landing page, authentication, and user dashboard.
  • Stripe subscriptions with at least two plans and usage limits.
  • Prompt → script → voice → video generation flow (vertical format, MP4 output).
  • Video history and downloads from the dashboard.
  • Basic admin view for users, plans, and usage.
Future additions like auto-posting and advanced analytics are planned for Phase 2+.

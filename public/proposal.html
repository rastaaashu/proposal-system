Project overview
Summary

Design, launch, and grow a high‑quality gambling YouTube channel under the brand OneTimeTV, focused on casino content with a consistent, recognizable visual style.

The goal is to build a monetizable channel in the next 1–2 months by combining your on‑camera casino sessions with my editing, branding, and YouTube strategy (CTR, watch time, retention, and consistency).

Scope & deliverables
Phase 1 – Channel Launch

Phase 1 covers everything needed to put OneTimeTV in front of viewers and aim for monetization:

  • Full channel setup: OneTimeTV branding, layout, and configuration.
  • Custom graphics: banner, logo/profile, thumbnail templates, intro/outro.
  • Editing of initial long‑form videos (8–15 minutes) from your casino footage.
  • Editing of multiple shorts (15–45 seconds) from each main video for growth.
  • Strategy for titles, thumbnails, descriptions, and upload schedule.
  • Basic analytics review after the first uploads to refine hooks and pacing.
Future phases can include live‑stream setup, sponsorships/affiliate funnels, and more advanced data‑driven optimization once the channel is monetized.
